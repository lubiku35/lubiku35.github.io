Project ČVUTNESS project is a combination of functional components including sports industry, application of modern technologies, the support of a healthy lifestyle and, last but not least, service for people and society.

ABOUT CVUTNESS

How the stadium looks now ?

Division of the Strahov stadium into several smaller stadiums holding major sports such as Football, Hockey, Basketball, etc... The second part is a section for the physically handicapped, which will contain a swimming pool and other movement aids. In this part, there will also be Wellness for everyone. The third and final part is a place for the merger of sports and technology, where the latest Virtual Reality technologies will be developed and tested, also there will be a section just for relaxation.
